## Key Observations from Sample Conversations

### HOT Lead Indicators:
- Immediate timeline ("I want to start immediately")
- Budget confirmed and realistic
- Documentation ready or nearly ready
- Clear business activity and plan
- Asking about next steps and quotations
- Decisiveness in responses

### WARM Lead Indicators:
- Timeline within 1-2 months
- Engaged and asking detailed questions
- Budget range identified but seeking information
- Needs comparison or more details before committing
- Responsive but not urgent

### COLD Lead Indicators:
- Just exploring / no clear timeline
- Budget concerns or unrealistic expectations
- Vague responses about business plans
- "Let me think about it" / "Maybe later"
- Early research phase with minimal commitment

### Bot Behavior Patterns:
1. **Progressive discovery**: Asks 1-2 questions at a time, never interrogates
2. **Context memory**: References previous answers naturally
3. **Empathetic handling**: Acknowledges concerns, offers alternatives
4. **Clear recommendations**: Based on gathered information
5. **Appropriate escalation**: Complex needs or high-value leads to human team
6. **Internal markers**: All business data tagged but stripped from customer view
